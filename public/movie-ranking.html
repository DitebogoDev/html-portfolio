The Best Movies According to Ditebogo

My top 3 movies of all-time

The Lion King

"The Lion King" is an animated Disney classic that follows the journey of a young lion named Simba, who, after the tragic death of his father Mufasa, must overcome adversity and reclaim his rightful place as the king of the Pride Lands.

The Expendables

"The Expendables" is an action-packed ensemble film featuring a group of elite mercenaries, led by Barney Ross (Sylvester Stallone), who undertake a dangerous mission to overthrow a ruthless dictator. Packed with intense combat scenes, the movie showcases the camaraderie and firepower of its star-studded cast, including Jason Statham, Jet Li, and Dolph Lundgren.

I am Legend

"I Am Legend" is a post-apocalyptic sci-fi thriller starring Will Smith as the last man alive in New York City, battling both mutated creatures and loneliness after a viral outbreak decimates the human population. As scientist Robert Neville, Smith's character struggles to find a cure for the virus while navigating the desolate urban landscape and confronting the haunting consequences of isolation.
